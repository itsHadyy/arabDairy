Vanilla Gourmet Cake

ingredients

3 tablespoons sugar
4/3 cup unsalted butter
8 Panda Gourmet cubes
2 and 1/2 cups brown sugar
6 large eggs
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
3 cups sifted flour
1 teaspoon salt

Steps

1: Preheat the oven to 162.778Â°C.
2: Grease the pan with oil and sugar from the inside and spread evenly. Place the butter, Panda Gourmet cubes, and brown sugar in an electric mixer bowl and beat on medium speed for 3 to 8 minutes until the mixture becomes light and creamy.
3: Add the eggs one at a time while continuing to mix well. Then add the vanilla.
4: Mix the flour and salt mixture in a medium bowl, then slowly add them to the butter mixture while continuing to mix at low speed, then beat with a spoon to ensure thorough mixing.
5: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
6: Bake in the middle of the oven for 60 to 70 minutes.
7: Let the cake cool in the pan for 30 minutes.
