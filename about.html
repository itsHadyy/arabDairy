Welcome to
Arab Dairy's Family

Arab Dairy has a promise for quality in all its different production phases to ensure final product quality by thoroughly testing raw materials and continuous testing throughout production phases. ISO 9001-2000 & HACCP procedures are in place to ensure wholesomeness.

Arab Dairy exports all of its brand names to the Arab & African countries such as Lebanon, Jordan, Libya, Saudi Arabia, Palestine, Bahrain, Kuwait, Syria, Yemen, Iraq, Mauritius, etc.

We genuinely believe that our past successes are in large part due to the diligent efforts of our team. We feel confident that our company will continue to go from strength to strength towards a future that is as successful as our past.
