Cheese Pizza

ingredients

Pizza dough, divided into two halves
Vegetable oil
Coarse cornmeal for dusting
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
4 cloves garlic, sliced
One and half cups Panda Mozzarella cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon fresh chopped oregano
2 cups shredded Panda Mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Arugula, for garnish

Steps

1: Preheat the oven to 260Â°C, and sprinkle a baking sheet with cornmeal-dusted parchment paper.
2: Heat the olive oil in a skillet, then add the garlic until golden, then remove and season it with salt. (Set it aside.)
3: Mix Panda Mozzarella cheese with salt, pepper, and half of the herbs in a bowl.
4: Shape each pizza dough ball, and transfer it to the baking sheet.
5: Brush the dough with garlic oil, then add the Mozzarella cheese.
6: Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted, for about 15-17 minutes.
7: Garnish with the remaining herbs, garlic, and arugula before serving.
