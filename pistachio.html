Pistachio Cheese Cake

ingredients

1/2 cup plain biscuit crumbs
70 grams cream cheese mixture butter
10 pieces of Panda Gourmet squares
1 can of cream
1 can of condensed milk
2 tablespoons pistachio cream
1 cup powdered pistachios
Sliced pistachios

Steps

1: Mix the biscuit mixture in a food processor or blender.
2: In a small cheesecake pan, place a small amount of the biscuit mixture, ensuring even distribution and pressing it down to make it flat. Then place it in the freezer for 10 minutes to cool.
3: In a blender, place all the ingredients for the cream cheese mixture and blend well.
4: Spread the cream cheese mixture over the biscuit base. Then bake it in the oven at 180 degrees Celsius for about 20 to 30 minutes.
5: Allow it to cool, then refrigerate for 30-45 minutes. Before serving, garnish with sliced pistachios.
